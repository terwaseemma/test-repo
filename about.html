Emmanuel Terwase

Emmanuel Terwase is a Nigerian entrepreneur and clean energy advocate passionate about creating positive change in his country. He is the founder of Boost Power Technologies, a company that builds and operates solar microgrids to provide sustainable electricity for communities in Nigeria.

Emmanuel's dedication to addressing socio-economic issues earned him the "Omo Sustainability Prize" at the 2018 Unilever Idea-trophy in Nigeria and the $10,000 Allon/Clean Technology Hub 2021 grant award. Emmanuel served as the Team Lead and President of Enactus FUTO, where he successfully led a team of over 100 students to execute various projects in energy, water purification, and other areas. He is also a 2019 alumnus of the Tony Elumelu Foundation Entrepreneurship Program.

Emmanuel is currently a Class of 2024 Entrepreneur in Training at MEST Africa and possesses skills in digital marketing, front-end development, sales, and more.
